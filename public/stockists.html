ABOUT US

SCIENTIFICALLY FORMULATED SKIN FOOD

Beauty Fix is a South African skincare brand established in 2014, it was developed by partners Ursula Uys and Danielle van Rooyen who are both very passionate about beauty and branding. After months of research and development in both the product and the brand, Beauty Fix was finally born.

Our products are scientifically formulated to feed the skin and are free of: alcohol, synthetic colours, fragrances, parabens, PEGs, TEA, DEA, sulphates, mineral oils and silicones. We also never test our products on animals.
